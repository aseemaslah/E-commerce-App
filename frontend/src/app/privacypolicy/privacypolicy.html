Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 28, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to E-Commerce. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services. By using our site, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: name, email address, phone number, billing/shipping address when you place an order or create an account.
  • Account Credentials: email and password (stored securely).
  • Payment Information: payment card details are processed by third-party payment gateways and are not stored on our servers unless explicitly stated.
  • Usage Data: IP address, device information, browser type, pages viewed, and other analytics data.
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies: to remember preferences, show personalized content, and improve site functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for purposes including:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services and the shopping experience.
  • To process transactions and send order confirmations, invoices, and shipping updates.
  • To communicate with you about promotions, new products, and changes to policies (you can opt out of marketing emails).
  • To detect, prevent and address technical issues or fraud.
  • For analytics and research to understand how users interact with our site.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies for site functionality, performance, and advertising. You can control cookie preferences in your browser; however, disabling certain cookies may affect the site functionality.

5. Sharing & Third Parties

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who perform services on our behalf (payment processors, hosting providers, delivery partners).
  • When required by law, regulation, or to respond to lawful requests by public authorities.
  • In connection with a business transfer such as a merger, sale of assets, or acquisition.

6. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data. No method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure—while we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights including access to your data, correction, deletion, and objection to processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond in accordance with applicable law.

8. Children

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

9. Links to Other Websites

Our site may contain links to third-party websites. This Privacy Policy does not apply to those sites — we encourage you to read their privacy policies.

10. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be communicated via the website or email when appropriate.
